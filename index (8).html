About the Event

An Intimate Evening of
Insight & Connection

We bring together approximately 70 Korean graduate students at Columbia with distinguished professionals across industries — for an evening of panel discussions, genuine dialogue, and warm networking.

🎤

Moderated Panel Discussion

A professionally moderated panel featuring multi-round discussions — from shared reflections to deep-dive topics to live audience Q&A.

🤝

Meaningful Networking

An extended dinner and networking session where students and speakers connect authentically over food and drinks in a warm atmosphere.

✨

Professional Venue

Hosted at the prestigious Ballroom at Columbia University Irving Medical Center — a space befitting the caliber of our speakers and attendees.

Program

Evening Schedule

A carefully curated flow — from structured panel discussion to organic connection.

6:00 PM – 7:30 PM
Part 1 — Panel Discussion
“Stories of Those Who Study the World” — A moderated panel where speakers share not just how they built their careers, but the choices, challenges, and lessons that shaped who they are.
Moderated Panel Multi-Round Live Q&A
▶
Opening (10 min)

MC introduces CU KGSA, sponsors, and the moderator. Each speaker is introduced to the audience.

R1
Round 1 — Common Questions (25 min)

Moderator poses shared questions to all speakers — uncovering pivotal moments and reflections from each career journey.

R2
Round 2 — Deep Dive (30 min)

Topic-based discussions on career choices, trade-offs, values, and work-life balance — with cross-panel dialogue.

R3
Round 3 — Audience Q&A (20 min)

Pre-submitted and live questions from the audience, curated and directed by the moderator.

■
Closing (5 min)

Moderator wraps up key themes. MC transitions to Part 2.

Our Community

CU KGSA is the representative organization for Korean graduate students at Columbia University. With over 900 members including current students and alumni, we foster a vibrant community through social events, professional development, and cultural exchange. Each year, we welcome approximately 200 new members and host a series of flagship events.
