About Us

Columbia University
Korean Graduate Student Association

CU KGSA is the representative organization for Korean graduate students at Columbia University. With over 900 members including current students and alumni, we foster a vibrant community through social events, professional development, and cultural exchange. Each year, we welcome approximately 200 new members and host a series of flagship events.
